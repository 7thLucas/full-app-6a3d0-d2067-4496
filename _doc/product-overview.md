# Product Overview — Learn w Angel

## What It Is
Learn w Angel is a Mandarin Chinese learning web app built around the official HSK curriculum (levels 1–6). Learners study vocabulary through interactive flashcards, hear native-quality audio pronunciation for every word, and stay motivated through a daily streak system.

## Users
- Mandarin language learners at all stages — beginner (HSK 1) through advanced (HSK 6)
- Students preparing for official HSK proficiency exams
- Self-directed learners who want structured, level-based vocabulary acquisition

## Core Features
1. **HSK Flashcards (all levels)** — Full vocabulary decks for HSK 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6. Each card shows Chinese character, pinyin, English meaning, and example sentence.
2. **Audio Pronunciation** — Native Mandarin audio plays on every card so learners hear correct tones as they study.
3. **Daily Streak Tracking** — Users earn and maintain a daily streak for each study session. Streak is displayed prominently to reinforce habit formation.
4. **Quiz Mode** — Multiple-choice quizzes (4 options) after study sessions to test vocabulary recall.
5. **Level Navigation** — Clear level selector (HSK 1–6) so users can jump to their current proficiency level or review lower levels.
6. **Progress Tracking** — Per-level progress: cards seen, cards mastered, accuracy rate.
7. **Dashboard Motivation Banner** — Configurable image + quote on the learner dashboard to reinforce daily habit.

## Brand & Tone
- Name: Learn w Angel
- Palette: Chinese red primary (#C41E3A), deep navy secondary (#1A2942), gold accent (#F5A623)
- Typography: Outfit (headings), Inter (body)
- Tone: Focused, encouraging, culturally respectful — serious about learning but not intimidating

## Strategic Principles
- Structure follows official HSK levels — no invented hierarchy
- Audio is non-optional; every card has sound
- Streak mechanic is the primary retention driver
- Mobile-first UI: flashcards work cleanly on phone screens
- No account required to start; progress is stored locally until sign-up

## Scope (Shipped)
- Flashcard study mode for all 6 HSK levels — real vocabulary seeded for every level (HSK 1–6)
- Audio playback per card
- Daily streak counter
- Level selector with per-level progress summary (words learned / total per level)
- Quiz Mode (multiple-choice, 4 options) per level
- Full auth flow (register, login, session)
- Admin panel for adding/managing vocabulary
- Dashboard motivation banner (configurable image + quote)
