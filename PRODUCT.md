# learnHSK

## What it does
A gamified Mandarin Chinese learning app covering all HSK levels (1–6+). Users study vocabulary through interactive flashcards with real audio pronunciation, track daily streaks to stay motivated, and progress level by level through the official HSK curriculum.

## Users
- Beginners starting with HSK 1
- Students preparing for HSK exams
- Self-study adults learning Mandarin at their own pace

## Core Problem
Existing HSK platforms are boring and fail to retain users. learnHSK solves this with gamification, audio-first learning, and streak mechanics.

## Brand
- Name: learnHSK
- Tone: Encouraging, focused, modern
- Anti-references: Dry vocabulary lists, no-feedback quizzes
